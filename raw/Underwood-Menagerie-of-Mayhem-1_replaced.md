Welcome to the Menagerie of Mayhem! Making 【敌人 (adversaries)】 to use with the 【《匕首之心》 (Daggerheart)】 rules is one of my favorite ways of engaging with the game, and so I'm delighted to be bringing you the first installment of this series as I continue to explore the possibilities of adversary design.

In this first volume, you'll find: a couple of multi-phase, boss fight-style 【独狼 (Solo)】 【敌人 (adversaries)】, the Blood Mage and the Young Thorn Dragon; 【重度伤害 (major)】 【社交 (Social)】 【敌人 (adversaries)】 like the Trickster Spirit and the Herald of Conquest; and some new adversary groups like the Grinning Legion and the Congregation of the End. I 【希望点 (hope)】 you enjoy using these foes!
